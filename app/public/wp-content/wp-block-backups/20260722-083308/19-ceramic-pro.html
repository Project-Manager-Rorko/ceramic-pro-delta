why Ceramic pro?

Ion Exchange Technology

During application, smaller ions in the base coat are replaced by larger ions in the top coat. This reduces the free space between molecules, creating a denser, stronger, and more resilient ceramic layer.

  • Unmatched scratch resistance
  • Extreme hydrophobic properties
  • Thicker layer than traditional 9H

Ceramic Pro ION.

Ceramic Pro ION is a two-step ceramic coating system utilizing advanced Ion Exchange Technology. It represents the next evolution in paint protection, offering superior strength and durability compared to traditional nanoceramic coatings.

STEP 01

Ceramic Pro 9H (Base)

The permanent foundation. 9H is a 3D ceramic molecular matrix structure that offers excellent scratch resistance & chemical protection. Multiple layers can be applied to increase thickness and durability.

STEP 02

Ceramic Pro Top Coat

The super-hydrophobic finishing layer. Top Coat is applied over the 9H base to provide extreme water and dirt repellency, ensuring the surface stays cleaner for longer and is incredibly easy to wash.

The Multi-Layering System

Ceramic Pro's unique formula allows it to be built up in multiple layers. This increases the thickness of the coating, providing ultimate protection and depth of gloss that single-layer systems simply cannot match.

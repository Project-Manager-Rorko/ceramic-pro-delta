What Is Composite Protection Film (CPF)?

Composite Protection Film (CPF) is an advanced vehicle protection system developed using patented Flexible Ceramic Composite Technology (FCCT). Unlike traditional Paint Protection Film (PPF), CPF structurally integrates advanced protection film technology with nanoceramic coating performance into a single protective system.

Boosted with Ceramic Pro ION coating and powered by Ion Exchange Technology, CPF delivers the combined benefits of paint protection film and ceramic coatings while overcoming the limitations of both.

The result is a stronger, glossier, more durable protective surface that enhances your vehicle's appearance while protecting it from environmental contaminants, scratches, stains, UV exposure, and daily wear.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Ceramic Coating process and post-care maintenance.

What makes Composite Protection Film different from regular PPF?

Composite Protection Film combines advanced protection film technology with integrated ceramic performance for enhanced durability, gloss, and surface protection.

Does CPF provide ceramic coating benefits?

Yes. CPF integrates ceramic technology to improve hydrophobic performance, gloss, stain resistance, and surface smoothness.

Does Composite Protection Film protect against scratches?

CPF helps protect against minor scratches, swirl marks, road debris, and daily surface damage.

How long does Composite Protection Film last?

Its lifespan depends on maintenance, driving conditions, and usage, but it is designed for long-term protection performance.

Can CPF be applied on a new car?

Yes. It is highly recommended for new vehicles to preserve paint quality from the beginning.

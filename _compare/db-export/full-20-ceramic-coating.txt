<!-- /wp:heading -->
</div>

<div class="wp-block-group cp-about-right">
<!-- wp:heading {"level":5,"className":"cp-text-lead"} -->
<h5>Ceramic coating is a high-performance liquid polymer applied to a vehicle?s exterior to create a strong, invisible protective layer over the paint. Once applied, it chemically bonds with the surface, making it far more durable than traditional waxes or paint sealants that wear off quickly.</h5>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:group {"className":"cp-text-columns"} -->
<div class="wp-block-group cp-text-columns">
<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This advanced coating helps protect the vehicle from UV rays, oxidation, dirt, chemical stains, water spots, and environmental contaminants. It also provides excellent hydrophobic properties, causing water and dust to slide off easily, making maintenance simpler.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>In addition to protection, ceramic coating enhances the vehicle?s gloss, color depth, and overall appearance, helping it maintain a showroom-like finish for years with proper care and maintenance.</p>

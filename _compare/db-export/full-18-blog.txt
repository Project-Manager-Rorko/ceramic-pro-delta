<h2>Introduction</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":"p"} -->
<p>Sports cars often operate under conditions that expose paint to road debris, dust, and sunlight. Protective technologies like Ceramic Coating and Paint Protection Film help preserve exterior finish and resale value.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":2,"className":"h2"} -->
<h2>Understanding Ceramic Coating</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":"p"} -->
<p>Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied to the paint and cured to form a hydrophobic and durable protection layer.</p>

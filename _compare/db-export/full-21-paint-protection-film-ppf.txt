<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:group {"className":"cp-text-columns"} -->
<div class="wp-block-group cp-text-columns">

The film is virtually invisible, preserving the original color and finish of the vehicle while maintaining its showroom-quality look.</p>

<p>With self-healing properties in many advanced films, minor scratches can disappear with heat, making PPF an effective long-term solution for maintaining your car's exterior and resale value.</p>
